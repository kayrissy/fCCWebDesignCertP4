Background

The NonIdeal Artist is a finance person by day; nerd, artist and nature enthusiast by nights and weekends. Most nights he spends playing video games, talking politics, or hiking but then a spark of creativity will suddenly hit the canvas in the form of acrylic paint (because its easier to clean up after).

The story begins back in 2016 when The NonIdeal Artist found and watched many Bob Ross videos on YouTube. He connected with Bob's fondness of nature and found his voice to be very calming.

The NonIdeal Artist currently focuses on nature paintings with trees, mountains, and bodies of water as preferred subject but ranges to space scenes whenever he feels like it.
